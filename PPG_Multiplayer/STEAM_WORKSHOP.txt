[h1]PPG Multiplayer[/h1]

[b]Play People Playground online with a friend.[/b] Share a 5-character room code and you're in the same sandbox together - see each other's cursors, spawn and build side by side, take control of each other's objects, and watch shots fly on both screens.

No IP addresses, no port forwarding, no server to run. It connects through a hosted relay automatically, so it just works.

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Room codes[/b] - host a room, share the code, your friend joins. That's it.
[*][b]Live cursors[/b] - see each other's mouse move in real time, each player in their own color with their Steam name.
[*][b]Shared spawning[/b] - anything you spawn from the catalog shows up on both screens.
[*][b]Take control[/b] - left-click another player's object to grab it, throw it, and mess with it.
[*][b]Shots sync[/b] - fire a gun and your friend sees the muzzle flash and tracer.
[*][b]Share your whole build[/b] - one click streams everything already in your scene to whoever joins.
[*][b]In-game chat[/b] - press Enter to chat with your friends, color-coded by player.
[*][b]Live ping[/b] - see your latency to the server at a glance.
[*][b]Solo self-test[/b] - a built-in test mode so you can check it works before a friend hops on.
[*][b]Clean in-game UI[/b] - a proper panel on the main menu and in the sandbox (press M). No console commands.
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h1]How To Use[/h1]

[b]First-time setup (do this once):[/b]
[olist]
[*]Subscribe to the mod and make sure it's enabled in the mod list. Your friend does the same.
[*]Open [b]Settings[/b] and turn [b]"Reject suspicious mods" OFF[/b]. The mod uses networking, so the game flags it - this is required.
[*][b]Fully quit and reopen[/b] the game (the setting only applies after a restart).
[*]If the mod tile shows "ERROR", click [b]Recompile[/b]. You should see "Multiplayer Mod Loaded!".
[/olist]

[b]Playing together:[/b]
[olist]
[*]Load any sandbox map. (The multiplayer menu wakes up once you've entered a map.)
[*]Press [b]M[/b] for the multiplayer menu, or use the [b]multiplayer[/b] button on the main menu.
[*]One of you clicks [b]HOST A NEW ROOM[/b] and gets a 5-character code. Click [b]Copy code[/b] and send it.
[*]The other pastes the code into the Join box and clicks [b]JOIN[/b].
[*]The status pill turns green - [b]Connected[/b] - and shows who's online. You're playing.
[/olist]

[b]In-game controls:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]M[/b] - show / hide the multiplayer menu
[*][b]Enter[/b] - open chat (Enter again to send, Esc to cancel)
[*][b]Left-click a friend's object[/b] - take control of it
[*][b]Solo self-test[/b] button - try it alone; a ghost cursor and duplicated spawns appear beside you
[/list]

[b]Want to see your friend's existing creation?[/b] While hosting, click [b]"Share my whole scene"[/b] and everything already in your sandbox streams to them.

[hr][/hr]

[h1]Good To Know[/h1]
[list]
[*]The relay is on a free host that naps after a while, so the very first connection can take up to ~30 seconds to wake ("Connecting..." then connects). After that it's instant.
[*]Ragdolls sync as one piece, so flailing limbs won't match exactly. Simple objects (crates, guns, swords, melons) sync cleanly.
[*]Some visual effects (fire, glow, electricity) don't fully transfer yet. More sync is coming.
[*]There's always a little network lag, so a friend's fast objects trail slightly behind yours.
[/list]
